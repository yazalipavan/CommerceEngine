# ADR-001 - Modular Monolith Architecture

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

CommerceEngine is starting as a single backend application. The system should remain simple while allowing future evolution into distributed services if business requirements demand it.

---

## Decision

The backend will adopt a Modular Monolith architecture.

Business capabilities such as Authentication, Products, Orders and Payments will be implemented as independent modules.

---

## Rationale

- High cohesion
- Low coupling
- Easier testing
- Easier maintenance
- Easier future migration to microservices

---

## Consequences

Each module owns its business logic.

Shared functionality will live under the shared layer only when required by multiple modules.

Cross-module dependencies should be minimized.
